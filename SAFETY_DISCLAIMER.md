# TeenDrive Safety Disclaimer

Created: 2026-05-12  
Creator: Vladimyr Merci

TeenDrive is a family driving awareness and coaching tool. It is not an emergency response service, crash detector, law-enforcement system, insurance telematics product, or legal driving record.

## Driver Responsibility

Drivers are responsible for obeying all traffic laws, road signs, posted speed limits, and safe driving practices. Drivers should never interact with the phone while driving.

## Detection Limits

TeenDrive uses iOS location, motion, protected-data, app lifecycle, and network signals. These signals can be unavailable or inaccurate because of:

- Missing permissions.
- Poor GPS signal.
- Low-power mode or system throttling.
- Network loss.
- Sensor noise.
- Delayed speed-limit lookup.
- iOS background execution limits.
- Phone unlock or app-open events that may not always mean handheld use.

## Parent Use

Parent alerts are meant to start a conversation, not to prove fault or assign punishment automatically. A reported event should be reviewed with context, especially when GPS or speed-limit information looks wrong.

## Emergency Use

TeenDrive does not call emergency services. In an emergency, call local emergency services directly.
